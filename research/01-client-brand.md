| Gap | Why it matters |
|---|---|

| No client logos / no testimonials | Competitors lead with Marriott, Google, Coca-Cola, Accra Mall |

## Brand Tone Assessment
Current: **utilitarian / under-confident.**
Voice is technically correct but feels small — there is no swagger, no positioning, no expertise framing. The "10 years" claim is the strongest asset but it is buried.

## Recommended Tone for Redesign
**Engineer-led, calm authority.** Confident without bluster. Specifications and numbers (BTU, COP, kW) used naturally. Ghanaian — proudly local but globally fluent. Think: an experienced engineer who lets the work speak.
